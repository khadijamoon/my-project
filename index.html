my first website

Bangladesh is a real river country, threaded together by over 700 rivers. In fact, three of the largest rivers in Asia flow through Bangladesh – the Ganges, the Meghna and the Brahmaputra. This makes for a lush green landscape, with an impressive diversity of wildlife.What is Bangladesh famous for? It is home to the world's largest river delta, which is formed by the Brahmaputra and the Ganges river. Roaming Bengal tigers in the Sundarbans, a mangrove and swampland in the delta. For the longest natural uninterrupted sea beach in Asia (Cox's Bazar beach), which is 150 km long.
